How to interpret these Question?

Some simple guidelines you can mention

1. Focus on the whole day

A single dish doesn’t define your health. Look at how many calories and nutrients you eat across the entire day.

2. Pair carbs with protein

Try combining dishes like dosa or idly with protein-rich sides such as sambar or chutney that contains lentils.

3. Enjoy, but balance

Biryani and pizza can still fit in if you balance them with lighter meals and regular activity through the week.
